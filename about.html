About Me

Biography

I am a graduate student at Indiana University Kelley School of Business studying Information Systems. I did my undergrad here as well, getting a bachelor's in Information Systems and a minor in Economics. I am originally from South Bend, Indiana (by Notre Dame) and went to Penn High School. Here, I played hockey for 4 years and did many extra-curricular activities.

Interests

Regarding my major, one of my biggest interests is cybersecurity. I love being able to identify loopholes or issues with current cybersecurity principles and finding ways to address those issues. I enjoy learning about different cybersecurity concepts related to cryptography. I also am interested network architectures. More specifically, how a network in a company is built and maintained. I enjoy the hardware aspects of these systems and how they operate together.
